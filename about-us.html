ABOUT US

Passion for cycling and love for the Bergamo territory

MARCO BIANCHI

Born and raised among the hills of Bergamo, I've transformed my passion for cycling into a mission: to help enthusiasts discover the lesser-known roads of my homeland.

For over 15 years, I've been exploring every corner of the Bergamo province on my bike, mapping routes, discovering breathtaking views, and selecting the best local food experiences.

With Secret Roads, I want to share this knowledge, offering personalized tours that combine sport, nature, and food culture.
